Xilinx ISE Design Suite Tutorial (Interactive Software Simulation + PDF)
University of Sistan and Baluchestan, Iran

ISE Design Suite is the Xilinx synthesis and analysis tool for some of its programmable devices, and it is widely used for academic purposes in Iran. Considering its popularity, I developed a Persian PDF tutorial based on my university curriculum to ensure that everyone can learn this powerful design tool.

In addition, most of the students from rural areas of my country had experienced internet access problems during the lockdown and were not able to download ISE software. Thus, I also created an online software simulation with a small download size to be uploaded on the university LMS.
